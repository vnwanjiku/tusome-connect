About Tusome Connect

At Tusome Connect, we are transforming the educational landscape in Kenya. Our platform bridges the communication gap between teachers and parents, ensuring better educational outcomes through technology.

We leverage tools like mobile apps, SMS, and USSD to foster communication in rural areas, enabling effective monitoring of student performance and involvement from both teachers and parents.

Our eco-friendly solutions are committed to reducing the environmental impact by eliminating the need for paper-based reports, further promoting sustainability in education.
