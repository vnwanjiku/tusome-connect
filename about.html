About Tusome Connect

Our mission is to bridge the communication gap between parents and teachers, enhancing student success across Kenya's rural and public schools. By leveraging digital tools, we aim to improve education accessibility, sustainability, and community engagement.

Our Vision

To empower schools, teachers, and parents with technology that fosters better student outcomes, reduces absenteeism, and strengthens the education system.

Our Impact

Inclusive Access

Ensuring every parent, regardless of location, stays informed about their child's academic progress.

Sustainability

Reducing paper waste by digitizing communication and academic updates.

Community Engagement

Fostering stronger relationships between schools and families through better communication tools.
