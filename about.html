About Tusome Connect

Tusome Connect is committed to bridging the communication gap between teachers and parents in rural and public schools in Kenya. We empower schools with digital tools that foster better student performance, reduce absenteeism, and encourage early interventions.

Our platform is eco-friendly, reducing the dependence on paper-based systems by digitizing communications and academic reports. We believe in creating a more inclusive and sustainable future for education in Kenya.
