About Tusome Connect

Tusome Connect is more than just a platform; it is a mission to bridge the communication gap between teachers and parents in rural and public schools across Kenya. Our goal is to enhance student performance, reduce absenteeism, and enable early interventions—all while fostering stronger school-parent relationships.

We leverage innovative digital tools such as mobile apps, USSD, and SMS to create an ecosystem that’s accessible and impactful. At the same time, we are committed to environmental sustainability by moving away from paper-based systems and embracing eco-friendly, digital communication.

Our Mission

To empower rural and public schools with digital communication tools that foster strong partnerships between parents and teachers, ensuring better educational outcomes for students.

Our Vision

To transform the education sector in Kenya by making communication more accessible, sustainable, and impactful, paving the way for a brighter future for students.
