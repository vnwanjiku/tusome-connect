Tusome Connect

Bridging education and communication across Kenya

Tusome Connect bridges the communication gap between teachers and parents in rural and public schools across Kenya. We empower student success, reduce absenteeism, and foster early interventions through mobile apps, USSD, and SMS.

We also champion environmental sustainability by digitizing academic updates and reducing paper waste, promoting a greener future for education.
